## Goal

Implement the revenue sharing model for paid marketplace listings. Compute publisher payouts based on listing revenue, platform commission, and usage-based metrics. Process monthly payouts and provide a seller dashboard for earnings tracking.

---

## Business Context

Revenue sharing enables a sustainable marketplace ecosystem. Developers are incentivized to build high-quality connectors when they can earn revenue from them. The platform takes a commission on each sale to cover hosting, payment processing, and marketplace operations. The revenue sharing model must be transparent, predictable, and pay out reliably.

---

## Technical Context

Revenue sharing works with the billing system (Phase 14). Paid listings generate revenue through one-time purchases or subscriptions. The revenue sharing service: (1) calculates the platform commission (configurable percentage, e.g., 30%), (2) computes publisher earnings, (3) tracks earnings in a `publisher_earnings` collection, (4) processes monthly payouts via Stripe Connect or manual payout, and (5) provides a publisher earnings dashboard.

## Requirements

### Functional requirements

- Compute platform commission on each listing transaction (configurable percentage per listing)
- Track publisher earnings per listing per month
- Support multiple payout models: fixed price per install, subscription revenue share, usage-based revenue share
- Process monthly payouts: calculate total earnings, deduct platform commission, initiate payout
- Integrate with Stripe Connect for automated payouts to publisher Stripe accounts
- Support manual payout for publishers without Stripe Connect (admin-triggered)
- Provide publisher earnings dashboard: total earnings, pending payout, payout history, per-listing breakdown
- Provide admin revenue overview: total marketplace revenue, platform commission, pending payouts, payout history
- Handle refunds and chargebacks: deduct from publisher earnings, negative balance tracking
- Generate payout reports (CSV) for accounting
- Send payout notifications to publishers (email + in-app)

### Non-functional requirements

- Payout calculations must be accurate to the cent (integer arithmetic)
- Monthly payouts must be processed within 3 business days of month end
- Earnings data must be immutable after the payout period closes
- Payout processing must be fully auditable

### Performance requirements

- Earnings calculation for a month: < 5 minutes for all publishers
- Publisher earnings dashboard: < 2s
- Payout processing: < 10 minutes for all publishers

### Security requirements

- Payout processing requires `admin:billing` permission
- Publisher Stripe account details must be stored securely
- Earnings data is publisher-scoped — publishers can only see their own data
- Refund handling must prevent negative balance abuse

### Accessibility requirements

- Earnings dashboard must have accessible data tables
- Currency formatting must respect locale

## Architecture Constraints

- All monetary amounts are stored in cents (integers) — never use floating point
- Platform commission is defined per-listing at publish time (configurable range: 10-50%)
- Monthly payout cut-off is the last day of the month at 23:59:59 UTC
- Payouts are processed on the 5th business day of the following month
- Stripe Connect Express is the recommended payout method for publishers
- Earnings records are marked as `pending`, `approved`, `paid`, or `disputed`

## Notes

Revenue sharing is a sensitive feature with financial implications. Test thoroughly with small amounts before enabling for real publishers. Consider implementing a "payout hold" period (e.g., 7 days after month end) to allow for refunds and chargebacks before finalizing payouts. The Stripe Connect integration requires publishers to have a Stripe account — provide clear onboarding instructions in the publisher dashboard.
