## Goal

Build the member management UI at `/settings/workspace/members` — a table listing all workspace members with their roles, status, and actions (change role, remove). Includes search, filtering by role, and pagination.

---

- apps/web/src/app/settings/workspace/members/page.tsx
- apps/web/src/app/settings/workspace/members/members-table.tsx

- apps/web/src/app/settings/workspace/members/change-role-dialog.tsx
- apps/web/src/app/settings/workspace/members/remove-member-dialog.tsx
- apps/web/src/actions/workspace/members.ts

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Members table shows: avatar, name, email, role, status (active/pending), joined date.
2. Admin/owner can change any member's role (dropdown with confirmation).
3. Admin/owner can remove any member except the last owner.
4. Role change shows confirmation dialog explaining permission changes.
5. Remove shows confirmation dialog.
6. Owner badge shown on owner's row.
7. Search filters by name or email.
8. Role filter (All, Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer).
9. Pagination for workspaces with >20 members.
