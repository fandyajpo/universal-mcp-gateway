# Step

Estimated Time: 1.5 hours

---

## Goal

Implement the invitation system: workspace admins can invite users by email, the invitee receives an email with an accept link, and the invitation status is tracked. Support for pending, accepted, declined, and expired states.

---

## Files To Create

- packages/database/src/models/invitation.ts
- packages/database/src/repositories/invitation.ts
- packages/auth/src/services/invitation-service.ts
- apps/web/src/app/api/workspaces/[workspaceId]/invitations/route.ts
- apps/web/src/app/api/invitations/[token]/route.ts (accept/decline)
- apps/web/src/app/settings/workspace/members/invite-dialog.tsx
- apps/web/src/app/settings/workspace/members/invitations-list.tsx
- apps/web/src/app/invitation/[token]/page.tsx (accept/decline page)
- packages/auth/src/emails/invitation-email.ts

---

## Requirements

1. Invite dialog: enter email(s), select role, optional message.
2. Validation: email not already a member, email not already invited (pending).
3. Invitation stored with: token, workspaceId, inviterId, inviteeEmail, role, status, expiresAt.
4. Invitation email sent with accept/decline links.
5. Accept page: if user is logged in, redirects to workspace. If not, shows login/register with redirect to accept.
6. Decline page: confirms decline, redirects to home.
7. Invitations expire after 7 days.
8. Invitation list in member management shows pending invitations with resend/cancel actions.
9. Rate limiting: max 20 invitations per workspace per day.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Invite dialog sends invitation and creates pending record.
2. Invitation email logs to console (dev mode) with accept/decline links.
3. Accepting with logged-in user adds them to workspace, redirects to workspace.
4. Accepting without being logged in redirects to login with post-login redirect.
5. Declining marks invitation as declined.
6. Expired invitations show "Expired" status.
7. Resend updates the `expiresAt` timestamp and resends email.
8. Cancel marks invitation as cancelled.
9. Inviting an existing member shows "Already a member" error.
